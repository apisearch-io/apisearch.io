Being a reseller

Our resellers are part of our team. That means that they are closely in touch with us though several communication tools, and they are part of our roadmap process.

Learn More
  • 1

    Learn how Apisearch work

    We help you to understand how Apisearch work in terms of technology, integration and administration. We update you about all new features before they are released. You are part of the team.

  • 2

    Integrate your clients or new ones

    Once you know exactly how Apisearch work, it's time to integrate your clients platforms or to reach new clients. You can specialize to one country and technology, and we will refer you clients when interested.

  • 3

    ¿Which is my revenue?

    We see you as one of our clients, including all your clients requests in one simple invoice. That means that you can go from a 0% of revenue (having one client), to +60% (the more clients and usage, the higher is that percentage)

Being a commercial

You can actively sell Apisearch as an external commercial. You will be trained and updated as our resellers do, but your revenue will be directly related to your closed clients.

  • 1 30% revenue the first year
  • 2 20% revenue the second year
  • 3 10% revenue the third year

Being an evangelist

Some of our partners are evangelists. You can talk about us, and you get paid for each lead referred by you and closed by us.

  • 1 20% revenue the first year
  • 2 15% revenue the second year
  • 3 10% revenue the third year
