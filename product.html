API Music

Search over millions of documents, and give to your users unique, amazing and unforgettable experiences.

Typo-tolerance

With Apisearch, no matter if your customers type wrong the word that they are looking for, the system will take care of this to give them the best result.

Multi-language search

Apisearch work with many different languages. Once you're creating the data index, you cat set the language you want to work with.

Multi-index search

Search multiple indices! you will be able to search over different data sets in the same result page.

Machine Learning

Apisearch learns from your users interactions, what they search and what they like. To give the best and curated results for them. Kind a personal shopper right?

Synonyms

Let your customers find what they are searching, no matter if they type a synonym or the exact matching word.

Geo search

Your data can be organized by its location. So you can easily build dynamic map searches or aggregate the results by the place where they come from.

Unlimited filters and aggregations

Allow your customers to refine their searches by color, rating, brand, type or whatever attribute you want. It's fully customizable and does't compromise the search performance.

Result boosting

Create boosting campaigns for specific items or searches. Increase the chance to give to your customers what they are searching to increase your site's conversion.

Real-time analytics

On Apisearch administrator panel you can check what your users search instantly or take a look to the past searches to know what is working, and what needs to be improved.

Customized search

Apisearch is highly customizable to fit the best to your product. From the shape of the item you want to index to the final user experience.

Premium infrastructure

With a worldwide data distributed network to serve your results at the lightning fast.
