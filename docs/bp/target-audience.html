Target Audience

Who you are selling to

As an expert business consultant, I have identified five target audiences for Apisearch. These target audiences include:

B2B

Small Business Owners

Small business owners are an important target audience for Apisearch. They often have limited resources and need a search engine solution that is affordable and easy to implement.
Profile
  • Owns a small online business
  • Has limited technical knowledge
  • Wants a search engine that is cost-effective
Preferences
  • Affordable pricing plans
  • Simple and intuitive user interface
  • Integration with popular e-commerce platforms
Frustrations
  • Difficulty in finding a search engine solution within their budget
  • Lack of technical expertise to implement complex search functionality
  • Inability to provide their customers with an efficient search experience
Behavioural Considerations
  • Limited budget
  • Limited technical knowledge
  • Focus on cost-effectiveness
Goals
  • Improve the search functionality on their e-commerce website
  • Increase customer satisfaction and conversions
  • Find an affordable and easy-to-use search engine solution

  • E-commerce events and conferences
  • Online marketing platforms
