Product and Services

Secondary Offerings

Additional products and services

In addition to our primary product of being a real-time search engine for e-commerce, Apisearch offers a range of secondary offerings to enhance the search experience and meet the specific needs of our customers. These offerings provide additional features and services that complement our core product and cater to a wider audience.

Enhanced Search Experience

Custom Product Recommendations

Our Custom Product Recommendations feature provides tailored product suggestions based on a user's browsing history and preferences, as well as the ability to create recommendations for a specific set of products. This feature enhances user satisfaction, drives additional sales, and boosts customer loyalty for e-commerce platforms.
Key Points
  • Custom recommendations based on user experience
  • Create recommendations for a set of products
  • Increased sales and customer retention
  • Integration with leading e-commerce platforms
Pricing Strategy
  • Monthly subscription based on the number of recommendations delivered
  • Additional fees for advanced customization options
  • Discounts for annual subscription plans
Challenges
  • Developing effective recommendation algorithms for product relevance
  • Managing a growing database of user preferences
  • Educating businesses on the potential of personalized product recommendations
Marketing & Promotion
  • Live demonstrations of personalized product recommendations at industry-specific events
  • Content marketing through blog articles showcasing the benefits of personalized suggestions
  • Targeted social media advertising for online retailers
Target Audience
  • E-commerce platforms
  • Online retailers
  • Websites with diverse product offerings
Execution Strategy
  • Refine recommendation algorithms to improve product relevance
  • Create marketing materials and assets to showcase the benefits of personalized recommendations
  • Collaborate with e-commerce platforms for seamless integration
