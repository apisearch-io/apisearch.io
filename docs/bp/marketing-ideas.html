Marketing Ideas

A list of ideas to market your business

As an expert business consultant for Apisearch, I have come up with 100 marketing ideas to help you attract new customers for your real-time search engine for e-commerce. These ideas cover various marketing channels and strategies, including content marketing, events, paid advertising, and more.

marketing

Blog

Create an informative blog series on the importance of effective search functionality for e-commerce websites.
Actions
  • Write posts about trending and unique content about our most important features
  • Deep into Conversational Search topic, as it's being trendy nowadays
Possible issues
  • Organic impact is a medium and long term work.
  • These actions will permanently depend on Google algorithm

Referal Program

Launch a referral program where existing Apisearch customers can earn rewards for referring new businesses.
Actions
  • There is a current program based on a lifetime revenue of 30% from referred clients
  • Short term benefits. Designed to ramp the company
Possible issues
  • This could change in the future, adjusting the percentage and the terms and conditions
  • Current plan can harm the company in long term if this action becomes a major adquisition channel

Free plan users

Launch a free plan for all these business that have low traffic impact, adding the powered by logo under the search layer
Actions
  • Adding a `powered by` logo increase the number of reversed links for Apisearch, improving the brand reputation
  • Increases the potential number of users that, potentially, can have a paid plan
Possible issues
  • This could cause tons of users without a paid plan, causing major issues in terms of infrastructure
