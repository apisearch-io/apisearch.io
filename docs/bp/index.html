Apisearch Bussines Plan

Detailed Apisearch business plan on November 2023, by Marc Morera

Apisearch is a real-time search engine for online stores, based on efficiency, the commitment to always deliver relevant results, and the use of artificial intelligence as an alternative to traditional keyword-based search.

Apisearch aims to embrace a scalable and efficient model, always striving to be at the forefront of developing new features related to online store search and building a product that helps companies convert more effectively.
