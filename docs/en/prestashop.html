Have questions?

If you have any questions, you can always reach out to us, but here are the answers to our most frequently asked questions

Is it compatible with my version of Prestashop?

Our Prestashop plugin is compatible with versions 1.7 and 1.8. If you are still using version 1.6, contact us, and we will help you.

Will I be able to add filters to my search engine?

Yes. Our Prestashop plugin will send us all your product values, and from our admin panel, you can create the filters you need. No programming knowledge required.

Will my users be able to search using synonyms?

Yes. In our admin panel, you can easily configure synonyms. You can download a list, complete it with new synonyms, and upload it quickly and conveniently.

Will I be able to see what my users are searching for?

Yes. In our admin panel, you can see the search performance, including what is being searched for, new sales opportunities, and even the conversion rate.

Can I try the search engine before paying?

Of course. Our main goal is for the search engine to help you sell more. Try it free for 60 days and decide whether to continue after seeing the results in your online store.

Will I get help setting it up?

Yes. At Apisearch, we are developing a knowledge base integrated with AI so that you are never alone. If you can't resolve your issues, there will always be someone available to provide support.
