Have questions?

If you have any questions, you can always reach out to us, but here are the answers to our most frequently asked questions

Do I need to install a plugin for the integration?

No. We only need your Google Merchant feed to integrate. Our platform can recognize all your products from the feed.

Will I be able to add filters to my search engine?

Yes. Our integration with the specified feed will send us all your product values, so you can create all the filters you need from our admin panel. No programming knowledge required.

Will my users be able to search for synonyms?

Yes. In our admin panel, you can easily configure synonyms. You can download a list, complete it with new synonyms, and re-upload it quickly and conveniently.

Will I be able to see what my users are searching for?

Yes. In our admin panel, you can see the search performance, including what is being searched for, new sales opportunities, and even the conversion rate.

Can I try the search engine before paying?

Of course. Our main interest is that the search engine helps you sell more. Try it free for 30 days and decide whether to continue after seeing the results in your online store.

Will I have help setting it up?

Yes. At Apisearch, we are developing a comprehensive knowledge base integrated with AI so that you are never alone. And if you still can't resolve your issues, there will always be someone available to offer support.
